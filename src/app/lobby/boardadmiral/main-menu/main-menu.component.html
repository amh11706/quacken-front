BA Interface Test

Shift click the map to select the nearest boat.

Ctrl click the map to paint multiple tiles in one click.

Ctrl + z to undo

Ctrl + y to redo

Flag coverage lets the bot figure out the best way to balance coverage and risk. They will only consider flags selected for them.

Tile coverage forces the bot to always end on the selected tiles (unless unexpected bump).

The last clicked tile will have a blue outline. All boats with coverage on this tile will have a blue border in the boat list.
