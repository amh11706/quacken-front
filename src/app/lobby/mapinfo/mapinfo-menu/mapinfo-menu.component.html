Map Info

Hotspot: A tile that covers the maximum flags (Warfrig).

-1 Hotspot: A hotspot that leaks 1 point from the maximum.

Spawn rating: How many spawn spots have the fastest route to a hotspot.

Hotspot rating: 1 point per hotspot, 0.5 per -1 hotspot.

Maneuverability: How many moves on average from a hotspot or -1 hotspot also land on a hotspot.

Spawns (green): Moves faster than the slowest spawn. +50 if it's the fastest.

Hotspots (red): 2 for hotspot, 1 for -1 hotspot.

Moves (blue): Number of moves from each hotspot or -1 hotspot that land on a hotspot without rock bumps.
